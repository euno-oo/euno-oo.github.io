Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Euno is designed with student privacy as a priority.

Data Storage

All data you enter in Euno — moods, diary entries, habits, tasks, notes — is stored exclusively on your own device using your browser's local storage. No data is sent to any external server, cloud service, or database.

No Accounts Required

Euno does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

Report Exports

PDF reports are generated entirely within your browser. Sharing these reports is entirely your choice.

Data Deletion

You can clear all stored data at any time via Settings › Clear All Data.
